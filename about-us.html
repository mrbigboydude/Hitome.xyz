About Us

Hitome Studios is a game development studio that specializes in creating high-quality games for the online platform BrickPlanet. The studio was founded in 2016 under the name Nexus Hub, with the goal of creating engaging and entertaining games for players of all ages. Over the months, Nexus Hub released several successful games on the BrickPlanet platform, earning a loyal following of players and fans. However, in March 2023, the studio announced that it would be discontinuing its operations, leaving many fans disappointed and wondering what would happen to their favorite games. Fortunately, the original owner and a group of passionate developers and fans of Nexus Hub decided to pick up the project and continue creating games under a new name: Hitome Studios. The new studio is committed to creating the same high-quality games that fans of Nexus Hub have come to love, while also exploring new and innovative ways to engage with players. With a talented team of developers, artists, and designers, Hitome Studios is poised to become one of the premier game development studios on the BrickPlanet platform. Whether you're a longtime fan of Nexus Hub or a newcomer to the world of BrickPlanet, we invite you to join us on this exciting new journey and experience the thrill of our games for yourself.
